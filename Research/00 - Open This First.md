# WCT Research Vault

This is the clean, non-duplicated vault structure.

## Start here

- [[00 Maps/Literature Notes Index|Literature Notes]] — one note for each paper
- [[00 Maps/Concept Index|Concepts]] — cross-paper concept notes
- [[03 Glossary/WCT Glossary|Glossary]] — alphabetical glossary
- [[00 Maps/Connections|Connections]] — paper-to-concept relationships
- [[00 Maps/Chronology|Chronology]] — publication order
- [[05 References/library|References]] — BibTeX and Zotero metadata

## Folder layout

```text
00 Maps/
01 Literature Notes/
02 Concepts/
03 Glossary/
04 Equations/
05 References/
06 Experiments/
07 Predictions/
08 Derivations/
09 Full Text/
10 PDFs/
11 Collections/
```

The Literature Notes, Concepts, Glossary, Maps, Collections, and reference data are populated. Equation, derivation, experiment, prediction, full-text, and PDF folders are present but require the actual PDFs for reliable extraction.

## Important

Open this folder as a **new vault**. Do not extract it over the earlier `Research` folder, because that creates duplicate folders such as `01 Papers`, `Concepts`, and `Literature Notes`.
