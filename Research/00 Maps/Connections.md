## Relationship types

- **Literature note → concept:** the paper uses, proposes, tests, or discusses the concept.
- **Concept → literature note:** every paper associated with the concept.
- **Concept → concept:** concepts that co-occur across papers.
- **Literature note → literature note:** papers with overlapping concepts or branch relationships.
