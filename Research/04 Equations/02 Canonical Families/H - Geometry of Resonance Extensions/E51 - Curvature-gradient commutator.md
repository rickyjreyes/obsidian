$$[\Theta, \nabla]\psi = \nabla\left(\frac{\nabla^2\psi}{\psi + \cdots}\right) - \frac{\nabla^2(\nabla\psi)}{\psi + \cdots}$$

("…" uses the same denominator structure as in $\Theta[\psi]$ from E17.)
