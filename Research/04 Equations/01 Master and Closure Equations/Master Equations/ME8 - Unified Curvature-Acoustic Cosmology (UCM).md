# ME8 — Unified Curvature–Acoustic Cosmology (UCM)

> [!info] Classification
> **Family:** Master Equation Architecture  
> **Status:** canonical  
> **Canonical source:** `MASTER_EQUATIONS.md`

## Canonical statement

The **WCT cosmology module (CM1–CM20)** is generated by a single coupled evolution system:

### UCM: Unified Curvature–Acoustic Cosmology

**Field Evolution:**

$$\partial_t \psi = -\Theta[\psi] \cdot J[\psi] + g|\psi|^2\psi + \eta\, \psi \circ \xi(t)$$

**Gravitational Potential:**

$$\Phi(k,t) = -C_\Phi \frac{\Theta(k,t)}{k^2}$$

$$\partial_t \Phi = -\gamma_h(k)\, \Phi$$

**Photon-like Mode:**

$$\dot{\delta}_\gamma = v_\gamma$$

$$\dot{v}_\gamma = -c_s^2(t)\, k^2\, \delta_\gamma - k^2\, \Phi - D_S\, k^2\, \delta_\gamma$$

**Matter-like Mode:**

$$\dot{\delta}_b = v_b$$

$$\dot{v}_b = -\frac{c_s^2(t)}{\mathcal{R}(t)}\, k^2\, \delta_\gamma - k^2\, \Phi$$

**Tight Coupling:**

$$\delta_b \leftarrow (1 - \varepsilon)\, \delta_b + \varepsilon\, \delta_\gamma$$

### Symbol Definitions

| Symbol | Definition |
|--------|------------|
| $\psi(x,t)$ | Primordial WCT field |
| $\Theta[\psi]$ | Curvature operator: $\Theta[\psi] = -\dfrac{\Delta\psi}{\psi + \varepsilon\, e^{-\alpha\|\psi\|^2}}$ |
| $J[\psi]$ | Nonlinear source (curvature feedback current) |
| $g\|\psi\|^2\psi$ | Self-interaction / NLS-like term |
| $\eta\, \psi \circ \xi(t)$ | Stochastic curvature forcing (Stratonovich) |
| $\Phi(k,t)$ | Curvature-generated gravitational potential |
| $\gamma_h(k)$ | Horizon-entry decay rate |
| $\delta_\gamma, v_\gamma$ | Photon-like curvature compression mode and velocity |
| $\delta_b, v_b$ | Matter-like compression mode and velocity |
| $c_s(t)$ | Curvature-derived sound speed |
| $\mathcal{R}(t)$ | Compression-to-radiation energy ratio |
| $D_S$ | Silk-like diffusion coefficient |
| $\varepsilon$ | Tight-coupling drag fraction |

### CM Equation Derivations from UCM

All cosmology equations CM1–CM20 are projections or reductions of UCM:

- **CM1:** take the $\psi$-equation alone ($g$, $\eta$ chosen per sector).
- **CM2:** compute $P_{\text{prim}}(k)$ from $\Theta[\psi]$ and its $k$-dependence.
- **CM3:** identify $\Phi(k,t) = -C_\Phi\, \Theta(k,t)/k^2$.
- **CM4:** define $\gamma_h(k) = |\partial_t \Theta / \Theta|$ and evolve $\Phi$.
- **CM5–CM7:** project UCM onto $(\delta_\gamma, v_\gamma, \delta_b, v_b)$ with $c_s(t)$, $\mathcal{R}(t)$, $D_S$.
- **CM8–CM13:** derive initial conditions and peak metrics from the same system.
- **CM14–CM20:** express interpretation, scaling, and expansion laws in terms of $\Theta$, $\Phi$, $c_s$, $\mathcal{R}$, $E_{\text{curv}}$, $E_{\text{grad}}$.
