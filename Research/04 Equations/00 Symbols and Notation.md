# Symbols and Notation

|--------|------------|
| $\psi(x,t)$ | Wavefield (complex) |
| $\Theta[\psi]$ | Curvature-feedback operator |

## Canonical disambiguations

- `σ_dens = κ² + τ²` has units `L⁻²`.
- `σ = √(κ² + τ²)` has units `L⁻¹`.
- `α` is sector-dependent; qualify it when ambiguity exists.
- `Θ[ψ]` denotes the regularized operator unless a paper explicitly uses the unregularized ratio.
